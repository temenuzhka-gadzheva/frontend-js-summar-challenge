TOKYO TOWER

Tokyo Tower, like the Eiffel Tower in Paris, is the symbol of the Japanese metropolis for which it receives its name. Over 150 million people have visited Tokyo Tower since the opening in 1958. Tokyo Tower is painted international orange and white which is required by the Japane air safety regulations.Tokyo Tower is the second tallest structure in Japan next to Tokyo Skytree.

In 1958, building was completed by Japan's oldest contruction company, Takenaka Corporation, and was opened to the public for the first time as a radio and television broadcasting tower. The project was conceived by Nihon Denpato(Nippon Television City Corp.) and was originally planned to be taller than the Empire State Building, which was the tallest building in the world at the time. Due to a lack of funding and materials, they changed the height to meet the minimum requirements for broadcasting TV signals through the Kanto region.
